Welcome to HCH

Sitting in the centre of Holmfirth, our independent coffee shop has been serving locally roasted artisan coffee from Bean Brothers and an exciting, contemporary menu since 2019. Open everyday, there is no need to book, just rock up and we’ll get you seated. Even your four legged friend is welcome along for a puppacino. Come along, kick back in the snug and soak up the awesome atmosphere at ‘Holme’ Coffee House. We can’t wait to meet you.
